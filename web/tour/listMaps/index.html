Ward Lock travel guides were tourist guide books to the British Isles and continental Europe published by Ward, Lock & Co. of London.

1946 Aerial (Pevensey Levels)

An RAF aerial photographic survey of Pevensey Levels and Barnhorn area. These surveys were carried out all over Great Britain under the name Operation Revue, they were intended to help revise maps, assist town planning and road building after WWII.

1942 Air Raid Precautions

This map shows fire action stations, emergency centres and warden posts around Bexhill during WWII. It was created by Borough Surveyor Harold Percy Storey in March 1942.
